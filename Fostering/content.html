Introduction to Fostering open day at Walthamstow Town Hall

22 March at 8.30pm

Foster care is a rewarding and life changing career, it also comes with a lot of challenges, We understand that you may have questions about what exactly it is you're going to expect with foster care, which is why we're here to help answer your questions!

Waltham Forest Information Session

5 April at 6.00pm

A presentation from the fostering team, one of our experienced foster carers and a young person that has grown up in care will be followed by an open question and answer session and then the opportunity to speak to the team and our foster carer individually.

Do you have the right skills to be a foster carer?

12 May at 2.45pm

If you’ve been thinking of becoming a foster carer but don’t think you have the right experience – think again. An informal chat from the fostering team and one of our experienced foster carers will explain what is needed.
