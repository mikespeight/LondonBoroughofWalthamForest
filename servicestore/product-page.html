Book a Handyman

book now
 

Do you need help getting odd jobs completed in your home, business or school?

We can help you quickly and cost effectively, so you can spend your time on more important things.

We have a team of reliable experienced professionals who can tackle a huge range of odd jobs for you, such as fitting shelving, fixing locks, assembling furniture and much more.

Our team are all trustworthy, reliable and skilled and bring all their own tools for the job.

 

Get your to do list done in no time with our highly-skilled team

We bring all our own tools – Don’t worry we don’t need to use that rusty hammer of yours we bring all our own up to date tools and safety equipment.

Fully insured team – our team are insured with £5 million in public liability insurance, so your home is in safe hands.

Highly skilled – Our team have many year's experience working for residents in Waltham Forest and beyond.

Available across Waltham Forest – We’re available for jobs across Waltham Forest.

Fair pricing based on time – We charge by the hour, with discounts the longer you book a handyman for.

Book now

Prices are done by time with a minimum of a 1 hour booking. After you have booked our team will let you know if they think you will need longer and help you book more time. Prices are inclusive of all taxes. For help with estimating how long a task might take see our pricing page.

Please note materials aren’t included in the price and will be charged separately or you can buy the materials needed yourself ahead of your handyman arriving.

Handyman Services

Here is a list of our services you can book by the hour:

  • Draught proofing
  • TV mounting or shelf fitting
  • Touching up paint
  • Moving furniture
  • Hanging mirrors, pictures or paintings
  • Easing jammed doors and windows
  • Assembly of flat pack furniture
  • Changing handles and locks
  • Pest proofing
  • Grab rails and minor adaptions for accessibility
  • Fitting hinges, handles and locks on cabinet doors
  • Fitting blinds, curtain rails and poles
  • Applying mastic and sealants

We can currently deliver our services across the Borough of Waltham Forest including; Cann Hall, Chingford, Friday Hill, Hale End, Highams Park, Leyton, Leytonstone, Walthamstow and Whipps Cross.

If you are from a neighbouring borough and are interested in our services please let us know by filling in our form. We will be in touch as soon as our services are available in other boroughs.
