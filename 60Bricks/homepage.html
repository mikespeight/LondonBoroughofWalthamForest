a place for everyone

Sixty Bricks is a dynamic and creative property development company wholly owned by the London Borough of Waltham Forest Council. We take our corporate responsibility seriously; we have a passion and deep conviction for challenging convention.

We ensure that a proportion of our homes are designated as ‘wheelchair accessible’ and are adaptable for multiple needs. Our approach means our homes are designed in such a way that the look and feel of each of our properties are developed the same, whether it’s rental or by private purchase form.

building communities

We are more than just a housing provider. We aim to improve the supply of affordable new homes and believe it is our duty to build socially inclusive and cohesive communities that thrive and inspire future generations.

We prefer to harness local talent and encourage our development partners and contractors to employ as many local people from within a 25-mile radius of each development as we can. We also ensure they have apprenticeship schemes which extend opportunities to young people to learn trades and skills whilst actively transforming their own communities.

We connect our homeowners and residents with the wider community through events and social media campaigns which encourage involvement and facilitate mutual recognition and support.

affordable homes

Increased demand for housing can make it harder to afford quality homes whether buying or renting. Our mixed-tenure approach helps to address the challenges of affordability by aspiring to offer up to 50% of our new homes with rents based on each individual’s affordability or by offering shared-ownership purchase opportunities on all our developments.

Our developments (including retail and commercial properties) may include parks, play areas, new road and street layouts which transform the feel and permeability of the area, and may also lend a specific and identifiable character to that area..

We are passionate about delivering high quality homes, great places where people love to live, and better outcomes for our residents. Waltham Forest is a vibrant and exciting Borough, which provides great opportunities for us to deliver an ambitious housing agenda. With Sixty Bricks we will successfully deliver hundreds of new homes..

Darren Welsh – Chair of Sixty Bricks Limited
