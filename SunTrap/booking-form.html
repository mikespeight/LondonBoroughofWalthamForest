Suntrap forest centre

Our Forest art activity is inspired by the work of landscape artist Andy Goldsworthy who uses natural material such as flowers, icicles, leaves, mud, pinecones, snow, stone, twigs and thornes to create temporary sculptures which draw out the character of their environment. After looking at examples, in the forest, small groups will create their own work of art using the natural world as their canvas.
