Phase 1 of The Hub revamp

We are excited to reveal phase 1 of The Hub website’s revamp. Alongside a fresh rebrand and new clean simple design, we have improved the menu functionality and search so that you are able to access information relevant to your position. We have grouped information based on your role within the school, so whether you are a governor, member of the senior leadership team, teaching or non-teaching support staff you can gain quick access to the essential statutory information and guidance.

New content has been added to provide clarity on what the statutory duty is, whose responsible and how you can ensure that your school is compliant. Please note that whilst we complete the transfer of information to the new Hub site some pages will still link back to the old site and so you will need to continue to use your existing logins to access them.

To ensure you don’t miss out on accessing the variety of continued professional development, that are available, all training and events will now be held in one central location on the  WF Traded Services site.

Over the next few weeks and months we will continue to update guidance and add new features. We hope you like the new website as much as we do.

Schools Financial Value Standard (SFVS) deadline

Governing boards of maintained schools must complete the SFVS standard assessment form annually. This information is then communicated to the ESFA by the local authority’s Education Finance team.

Please ensure that your returned form is signed by the Chair of Governors and returned to  EducationFinance@walthamforest.gov.uk by Friday 29 March 2019.

To access the SFVS assessment form and guidance on completing this please visit the Schools Financial Value Standard page.

Access Education budgets and workspace licence (previously HCSS)

The Education Business Effectiveness now hold an local authority's umbrella licence for maintained schools for Access Education Budgets & Workspace licence (previously HCSS).

Please ensure that your returned form is signed by the Chair of Governors and returned to  EducationFinance@walthamforest.gov.uk by Friday 29 March 2019.

To access the SFVS assessment form and guidance on completing this please visit the Schools Financial Value Standard page.
