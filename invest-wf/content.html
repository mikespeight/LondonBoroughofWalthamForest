  • Blackhorse Road

    Housing Project

    Covering 443 hectares Blackhorse Lane is a signifcant regeneration area in Waltham Forest. As a GLA Housing Zone the area has capacity for over 2,500 new homes, 1,000 new jobs and 15,000 sq metres of new business and retail space.

    Only 15 minutes by train from Central London this area has a unique location adjacent to Walthamstow Wetlands, London’s Nature Reserve - 211 ha of free-to-access public space on the doorstep of the growth area, opening in 2017.

    It has a growing creative and cultural business sector and the award winning Blackhorse Workshops is located at the heart of the area, with new creative workspaces planned at Lockwood Way and Uplands – a joint venture from the Council and the GLA from the London Regeneration Fund.
