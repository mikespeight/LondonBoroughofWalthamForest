AN ELEGANT VENUE

The Town Hall has elegant and stylish wedding venues.  It is the perfect place for couples who dream of an intimate wedding party in a grand, impressive venue.

Outside, guests will be awed by the breath-taking and photo-friendly fountain, expansive green and imposing architecture. Inside, a marble staircase leads you to striking rooms with vast windows that allow natural light to flood in. This is the place dreams are made of.
