Hungry Hedgehogs at Walthamstow Wetlands

Saturday 28th October 2017 <br>10am – 3:00pm

Join us at Walthamstow Wetlands for a fun, family friendly day with storytelling, talks and arts & crafts all about hedgehogs.

Discover the secrets of these amazing mammals and learn about how we can look after them. Why not create your own 3D hedgehog or have a go at making a hedgehog home for winter?
 
All welcome to this free event.

Family Fun Day - Hibernation at Walthamstow Wetlands

Tuesday 24th October 2017 10am – 3:00pm

Activity Days – Fun Events for under 5s at Walthamstow Wetlands

Every Monday and Wednesday from 23rd October- 20th December 2017 10am – 3:00pm

Join us at Walthamstow Wetlands for storytelling, wildlife activities and Crafternoons on different nature themes each Monday and Wednesday.

Enjoy creative stories about birds and mammals, create your own leaf prints and willow wands or track down minibeasts and spot birds. What adventures will you and your family have?

All welcome to this free event.

Hungry Hedgehogs at Walthamstow Wetlands

Saturday 28th October 2017 <br>10am – 3:00pm

Join us at Walthamstow Wetlands for a fun, family friendly day with storytelling, talks and arts & crafts all about hedgehogs.

As part of National Mammal Week, come and discover all about hibernation. Drop-in for a morning of storytelling and arts & craft activities or book in the afternoon for a home-building workshop. Can you help us make a building for bugs or a house for a hedgehog?
